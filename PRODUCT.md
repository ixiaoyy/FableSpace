# Product

## Register

product

## Users

FableSpace serves explorers who enter location-anchored AI spaces, spacekeepers who create and maintain those spaces, and NPCs as the configured experience layer. Explorers need to discover a nearby or shared space, understand what to do in the first minute, interact with NPCs, and return to remembered relationships. Spacekeepers need control over coordinates, NPCs, gameplay, access rules, and LLM configuration.

## Product Purpose

FableSpace is a real-map-anchored AI space UGC platform. Success means a visitor can move from a real coordinate into a space that feels authored, inhabited, and returnable, while owner-authored content, privacy boundaries, and SillyTavern compatibility remain intact.

## Brand Personality

Spatial, intimate, and sovereign. The product should feel like entering a living place rather than operating a generic chatbot list or a traditional map app.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a route-planning map app, a public social network, a combat RPG, a platform-token marketplace, or an AI system that publishes canonical space content without owner confirmation. Avoid generic SaaS decoration that hides the coordinate -> space -> NPC -> memory loop.

## Design Principles

1. Make the real coordinate and space anchor visible before adding fantasy.
2. Put owner-authored space and NPC content ahead of platform automation.
3. Let NPCs guide, host, remember, and create a first-minute path without taking agency from the visitor.
4. Separate public space payload, owner-private configuration, and visitor-private runtime state.
5. Favor living continuity, memory, and revisit cues over one-off chat.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Interfaces should remain usable on mobile and narrow screens, preserve keyboard-accessible controls, provide readable contrast, respect reduced-motion preferences where motion is introduced, and avoid exposing private visitor or owner data in public UI.
