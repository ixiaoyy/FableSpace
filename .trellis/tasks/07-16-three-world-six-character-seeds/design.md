# Content and Seed Design

## Space 1: 长明宫·雪夜诏书

- Incident: the emperor is unconscious and the bedchamber is sealed until the fifth-watch court meeting.
- Pair: chief eunuch 魏观海 controls the seal and doors; princess 萧明珠 intends to force entry.
- Visitor value: an ancient beggar knows unregistered routes and can move beneath palace notice.
- Decision: who opens the door, what happens to the unreleased edict, and who bears the cost of the princess's stolen half-token.

## Space 2: 青槐驿·狐火借命

- Incident: fox spirit 绯月 brings a medicine made from her fox core to save scholar 宁怀书 before sunrise.
- Pair: she wants to repay life with cultivation; he refuses to turn a moment of kindness into a lifelong debt.
- Visitor value: the begging bowl can hold moonlight water and buy one hour, not erase the cost.
- Decision: drink, refuse, delay, or search for a third path after both parties understand the price.

## Space 3: 临川大学·最后一堂课

- Incident: teacher 沈清禾 has accepted suspension for a leaked exam; rich student 顾野 caused the breach and can frame an innocent worker.
- Pair: her gentleness risks becoming enabling; his money and impunity collide with the first adult whose respect he values.
- Visitor value: the ancient beggar is outside modern campus status and money conventions.
- Decision: confession, evidence disclosure, substitution, or a bounded final chance before the morning hearing.

## Seed Encoding

- Existing schema only: `_tavern`, `_character`, `_world_info`, and `gameplay_definitions`.
- System-owned seeds use the local rules backend for no-network fallback.
- New character IDs intentionally have no dependency on retired public-welfare IDs.
- Portraits may temporarily use frontend archetype fallbacks; formal launch art is gated by the QA/cleanup children.
